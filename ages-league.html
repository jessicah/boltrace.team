Ages/Masters Racing League

Event Information

  • Start Date: 9th April, 2021
  • End Date: 28th May, 2021
  • Event Day & Time: Friday, 6:30PM UTC
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Results will be split into Masters 5-year category bands
  • Categories
    In the pen, these are based on your birth year, not your birthday (women add 10 years):
    1. 30 to 39
    2. 40 to 49
    3. 50 to 59
    4. 60 to 69
    5. 70 and OVER
  • Race Rules
    • Riders going over 5W/kg at 95% of 20 minute power will be removed from results.
    • Riders going over 6W/kg for 5 minute power will be removed from results.
    • Heart rate monitors advised to be worn by all participants.
    • Only powermeter and smart trainer users eligible for official result placement.
    • All bikes permitted.
    • No powerups permitted.
    • Official results located at https://boltrace.team/results/current/ages-league.
  • Points
    • Points available depend on number of finishing riders; please refer to our points table for full details.
    • All participants finishing outside of the top 50 score 1 point.
    • Points scoring applied to each category.
  • Points Rules
    • Points can only be earned in the correct category a participant races in.
    • Racing in an incorrect category will void any points accrued.
    • Points will not carry over when a rider changes age during a current league.
    • Best 6 of 8 rounds count towards final standings.
