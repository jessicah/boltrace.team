Fierce Felines Racing Series

Event Information

The Fierce Felines Racing Series is a series created by the BRT Hellcatz, providing hard racing for women across three timezones. We believe that women need more opportunities to race against women, and that races should be as equally hard as some of the top mens racing in Zwift. This series provides races that should cater to all abilities, with time trials, points races, fairly flat road races, and hilly road races.

We will be using ZwiftPower's womens categories for all races.

Race Rules

  • Women only. Shared accounts not eligible for official results.
  • Must be registered with ZwiftPower.
  • Riders exceeding 5W/kg FTP efforts will be investigated, with possibility for DQ.
  • zPower is not eligible for results.
  • Powerups will be awarded at every banner, 25% possibility of Leaf, Van, Aero and Burrito.
  • Race Commissaire's decision will be final.

Individual Points

  • Points will be allocated per category.
  • Points for the first 25 starting at 50 points.
  • 1 point will be awarded for those outside of the top 25.
  • If you enter more than one event in a weekend, only your best result will count.
  • Your overall score for the series will be the sum of your best 6 races.
  • For the criterium, finish order will be determined by sprint points, and then race points determined from this finish order. Please see an example points race breakdown for how this works in practice.

Team Points

  • Each team will score the sum of their top 3 individual rider points of the weekend.
  • There will be separate podiums for teams in each category.

Mid-League Upgrades

  • Riders going over category limits will be automatically upgraded in the official results.
  • When a rider has been automatically upgraded, they are expected to race in their new category for the remainder of the series.
